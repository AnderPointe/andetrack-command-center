# Phase 10 — Customer Demo Scripts

## 5-minute demo
1. Hook: "Show me one load from request to POD."
2. Customer submits via portal.
3. Dispatcher converts → AI recommends driver → one-click offer.
4. Driver accepts on phone → EliteNav turn-by-turn.
5. Customer sees live tracking. POD lands in portal.
6. Close: "That was 5 minutes. Now imagine 100 loads a day."

## 15-minute demo
Adds:
- Predictive risk + draft customer update with human approval.
- Executive ops health score.
- Audit trail showing every AI + user action.

## 30-minute demo
Adds:
- Integration Hub (EDI 204/214, webhooks, broker connectors).
- Security Center (RLS coverage, tenant isolation).
- Pricing + pilot conversation.

## 60-minute deep-dive
Persona walkthroughs: Owner, Admin, Dispatcher, Driver, Customer, Executive.
Includes Q&A and live ROI calculator.

## Discovery questions
- How many loads per day?
- How many drivers / dispatchers / customers?
- What's the cost of a late delivery?
- How much time do dispatchers spend on status calls?
- What systems must we integrate with (TMS, EDI, broker)?
- Who approves AI-driven customer updates today?
- What's your current customer visibility experience?

## Follow-ups
- Send tailored ROI run.
- Send security pack.
- Schedule technical deep-dive on integrations.
- Propose 30/60/90-day pilot.

## Objections (full bank — see /launch/sales)
TMS overlap, driver privacy, AI risk, security, CDL/non-CDL, customer portal,
integrations, implementation timeline, pricing.
