# Phase 10 — Customer Demo Scripts

Every Anderoute demo follows the same spine: **request → dispatch → driver → AI → POD → exec**.
The length only changes how deep we go on each step.

## Demo principles

1. **One real load, end-to-end** — never jump between disconnected screens.
2. **Show the AI explaining itself**, not just predicting.
3. **Keep the dispatcher in control** — every AI action requires approval.
4. **Tie every feature to a customer-felt outcome** (status calls down, OTP up, etc.).
5. **Close with the ROI calculator using their numbers.**

---

## 5-minute demo — "One load, request to POD"

| # | Stage             | What we show                                                   | Why it matters |
|---|-------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------|----------------|
| 1 | Customer portal   | Shipper submits a same-day request                              | Self-serve replaces email |
| 2 | Dispatch inbox    | Request becomes a routable load                                  | One queue, no toggling |
| 3 | AI recommendation | CoPilot scores nearby drivers + explains the pick                | Explainable AI |
| 4 | One-click offer   | Load offered with full audit                                     | Speed + governance |
| 5 | Driver app        | Push → accept → EliteNav truck-aware route                       | Driver experience |
| 6 | Customer tracking | Shipper sees live position without logging in                    | Stops status calls |
| 7 | POD               | Photo + signature lands back in the portal                       | Visibility loop closes |

**Close line:** "That was 5 minutes for one load. Now imagine 100 loads a day on the same rails."

## 15-minute demo

Add three moments on top of the 5-minute flow:

- **Predictive risk + draft customer update with human approval** (step 9 of the guided demo).
- **Executive ops health dashboard** updating live as the load completes.
- **Audit trail** showing every AI suggestion, approval, and data change.

## 30-minute demo

Add depth on what enterprises buy:

- **Integration Hub** — EDI 204/214, webhooks, broker + TMS connectors.
- **Security Center** — RLS coverage, tenant isolation, SOC 2 readiness.
- **Pricing + pilot conversation** — walk the 30/60/90-day tracks.

## 60-minute deep-dive

Persona walkthroughs in order:
**Owner → Admin → Dispatcher → Driver → Customer → Executive.**
Includes objection handling, live ROI calculation with their data, and a
proposed pilot plan before they leave the call.

---

## Discovery questions (top 8)

1. How many loads per day, and what equipment mix?
2. How many dispatchers / drivers today, and what's the growth plan?
3. What's the cost of one late delivery (penalty + relationship)?
4. How much time do dispatchers spend on status calls each day?
5. Which TMS / GPS / EDI / broker systems must we integrate with?
6. Who approves customer-facing updates today, and how fast?
7. What's your current customer visibility experience like?
8. Do you have SOC 2, SSO, or data-residency requirements?

## Follow-ups (within 24h)

- Tailored ROI run using the prospect's own numbers.
- Security pack (SOC 2 readiness, RLS model, audit trail brief).
- Technical deep-dive on the specific integrations they need.
- Pilot proposal — recommend 30 / 60 / 90-day track and success metrics.
