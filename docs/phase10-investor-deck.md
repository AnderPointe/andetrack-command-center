## Speaker-note rules

- Lead every slide with **one customer-felt outcome**, not a feature.
- For any AI claim, name the **human-in-the-loop control** in the same breath.
- Mark all forward-looking numbers as **illustrative until pilot data confirms**.
- End every meeting on slide 26 — never on pricing.
